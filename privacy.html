Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 11, 2026

1. Introduction

Purple Squirrel Media LLC ("we," "us," or "our") operates purplesquirrelmedia.io, a Web3 video streaming platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website or join our waitlist.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide:

  • Email address, if you sign up for our waitlist. Your email is stored locally in your browser via localStorage and may be transmitted to our systems for waitlist management.

Information collected automatically:

  • IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, and pages visited.
  • Cloudflare analytics data, including page views and visitor counts (aggregated and anonymized).

Cookies and similar technologies:

  • Cloudflare may set cookies for security, performance, and analytics purposes.
  • We use localStorage to persist your waitlist email on your device.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To manage the waitlist and notify you about platform launch updates.
  • To monitor and improve site performance and security.
  • To analyze aggregate traffic patterns via Cloudflare analytics.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Cloudflare: Content delivery, DNS, DDoS protection, and web analytics. Cloudflare may process your IP address and set cookies. See Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Fonts: If used, font files may be loaded from Google servers, which may log your IP address. See Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Retention

Waitlist email addresses are retained until the platform launches or until you request removal. Cloudflare analytics data is retained according to Cloudflare's data retention policies. localStorage data persists on your device until you clear your browser data.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate personal data.
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data.
  • Portability: Request a machine-readable copy of your data.
  • Objection: Object to certain processing of your personal data.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email address below.

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to individuals under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Your continued use of the site after any changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
