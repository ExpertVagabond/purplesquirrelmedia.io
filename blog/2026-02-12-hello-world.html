Hello World — Purple Squirrel Media is Live

Feb 12, 2026 · purplesquirrelmedia.io

After months of building in the background, Purple Squirrel Media finally has a proper home on the web. This post is a quick hello and a map of what we're working on.

What is Purple Squirrel Media?

We're an indie tech publisher and developer studio. That means we build developer tools, publish real books, ship mobile apps, and release open source code. No venture capital. No growth hacking. Just tools that work and words worth reading.

What we're building

Here's what's live or in active development:

  • NorthCatch — An AI-powered fishing companion for iOS, built with Expo and React Native. Species identification, weather integration, and catch logging.
  • Coldstar — Air-gapped Solana cold storage on ESP32 microcontrollers. Hardware-level crypto security with zero internet exposure.
  • Ruby Toolkit Suite — 163 CLI commands across 16 repos for DevOps, database management, deployment, and monitoring.
  • Local AI Stack — Run Claude Code 100% offline with Ollama, custom quantized models, and zero-API local development.

What we've published

Two books are live on Amazon, Google Play, and Apple Books:

  • Roger Ramjett and S.T.A.R. by Steven Karsten — a sci-fi adventure about courage and the bonds forged across the stars.
  • The Crypto Survival Guide — the definitive self-custody manual for securing your own crypto with hardware wallets, seed phrases, and operational security.

What's next

More tools, more books, more open source. This blog will be where we share what we learn along the way — the technical decisions, the shipping stories, and the occasional rabbit hole. No fluff, no filler.

Ship it. Own it. Keep going.

Thanks for reading. If you want to follow along, the GitHub is always open and the Twitter stays active.
